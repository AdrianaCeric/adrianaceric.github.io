Animating Yonge-Dundas Square: Summer 2021




Context

The Yonge-Dundas Square Board of Management manages Yonge-Dundas Square to enhance the vitality of downtown; to launch, promote, and operate Yonge-Dundas Square as an exciting commercial space born from the passion of its community and the energy of commercial participation, so as to develop a positive perception by way of its activities, security, and cleanliness.

Their team invited the nonprofit I'm involved in, Urban Minds, to "animate the Square for the summer, as a COVID safe community hub."

The question

My team a meeting pondering over this:

How might we create an interactive and safe place for the community to gather, relax, and have fun at Yonge-Dundas Square?

Timeframe

May 2021
(3 weeks)

Tools

Figma
Google Jamboard

Role

Brainstormer/Designer

Scope/Considerations

Certain parameters had to be set in place with our current design:

  • COVID-safe = Limit number of people gathering together
  • Overall vision = lounge/ backyard with dining areas and seating for people to gather, relax, and have fun
  • The Square is a public open space and event venue
  • Appeal to residents and tourists alike
  • Fun, visually interesting, bold. (Bold, bright colours)
  • A place to appreciate the outdoors
  • Interactive, playful
  • Flexible, mobile, detachable to accommodate events simultaneously
  • Maintain flexibility to adapt should restrictions change
  • Reflect diversity, inclusivity, and community
  • Stage area is an opportunity
  • Fountains to be operational
  • Maintain space along Yonge St for potential marketing activations
